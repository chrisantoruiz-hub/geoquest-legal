Privacy Policy for GeoQuest

Last updated: 29 April 2026

Overview

GeoQuest is a geography quiz game developed by Chrisanto Ruiz. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data Stored Only on Your Device

The App saves your quiz scores, level progress, daily streak count, and the date you last played using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you have iCloud backup enabled.

Game Center (Optional)

If you sign in to Game Center, Apple collects your alias, profile photo, and leaderboard scores. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. You can opt out by not signing in.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We receive only a confirmation that the purchase was made — no payment details, name, or Apple ID.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, IP address)
  • No analytics or advertising SDKs
  • No cross-app or cross-website tracking
  • No data is sold to third parties

Children

GeoQuest is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Deletion

All on-device data is deleted when you uninstall the App.
